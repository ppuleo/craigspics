About CraigsPics

Hey, remember when you could browse the images in a craigslist forum by searching for "<img*"? Besides being a perfectly legitimate method for performing a visual search, it was way more entertaining than scanning through hundreds of lines of indented text. You get it.

Since the craigslist department of fun suppression removed this feature, here's a quick and dirty workaround. CraigsPics is an old school web scraper built on bootstrap using a caching proxy to minimize the load on craigslist. You can download the source and run your own copy or use this one (until it's blocked).

But Why?

Relax, CraigsPics was built as a programming experiment, mostly out of boredom. Plus, most of the craigslist forums are like mini R-rated versions of imgur. Isn't that reason enough?

Is this OK with craigslist?

Nope. Repurposing craigslist content for any reason pretty clearly violates the terms of use.

Happy browsing.
